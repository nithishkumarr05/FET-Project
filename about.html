About Laptop Management

Welcome to Laptop Management, a simple web application designed to help you organize and keep track of your laptop inventory.

Features:

  • Efficiently add new laptops with their brand, model, and price.
  • View the list of laptops you've added.
  • Easily manage and update laptop information.

How to Use:

  1. Navigate to the "Add Laptop" section.
  2. Enter the brand, model, and price of the laptop.
  3. Click "Add Laptop" to include it in your inventory.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for each laptop you want to add.
  5. To view the list of added laptops, scroll down to the "Laptops" section.
